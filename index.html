Live a Healthy Lifestyle!

Wellbeing is not just the absence of disease or illness. It's a complex combination of a person's physical, mental, emotional and social health factors. Wellbeing is strongly linked to happiness and life satisfaction. In short, wellbeing could be described as how you feel about yourself and your life.

Nutrition

Its important to eat a healthy and balanced diet by making better food choices resulting in improved mental and physical health.

Sleep

During sleep your body is working to support healthy brain function and physical health. It promotes growth and development in children.

Exercise

Being physically active can improve your general health, help manage weight, reduce risk of disease and strengthen bones and muscles.

Meditate

Relaxing your body and mind is also very important, being calm and at peace can benefit your emotional wellbeing and reduce stress.

Sleeping Tips

  1. Create a cool and quiet sleeping environment
  2. Use ear plugs and face mask to block out external distractions
  3. Limit use of electronics before bed
  4. Maintain consistent sleep sqedule for 7 days a week
  5. Limit caffeine use at least 6 hours before bed
  6. Avoid alcohol and large meals before bed
  7. Reserve your bed for sleep only
